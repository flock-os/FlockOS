A Touch of the Gospel

Explore the depth of Scripture through interactive theology, biblical lexicon, character studies, counseling resources, self-assessments, and prayer — all in one place.

1. The Great Invitations

Jesus issued personal calls characterized by grace rather than performance. Click each card to uncover the profound hope offered to the exhausted, the thirsty, and those seeking intimacy.

2. The “I AM” Declarations

In the Gospel of John, Jesus uses seven metaphors to declare how He perfectly meets the deepest needs of the human soul. Select a declaration to explore His identity.

3. The Finished Work

Hope is not just found in what Jesus said, but in what He accomplished. Select an event from the timeline to understand the historical progression of His redemptive mission.
